COMMUNITY SERVICE PROJECT

Weekly Activities

Week 1 – Tree Plantation Drive

In the first week, our team conducted a tree plantation drive in nearby villages. The objective was to promote environmental sustainability and create awareness about the importance of greenery.

Week 2 – Village & Waste Management Cleanliness Drive

During the second week, we organized a village cleanliness and waste management drive. The activity focused on cleaning public areas and educating people about proper waste disposal.

Week 3 – Helping Poor People

In the third week, our team helped poor and needy people by providing essential support. This activity aimed to encourage social responsibility and empathy.

Week 4 – Student Awareness Camp at Sri Sairam Junior College

The fourth week involved conducting an awareness camp at Sri Sairam Junior College. Students were educated about community service, social responsibility, and environmental protection.

Week 5 – Nursery Visit & Learning About Plant Breeds

In the final week, we visited a nursery to learn about various useful plant breeds. This activity helped us understand plant varieties and their benefits.
